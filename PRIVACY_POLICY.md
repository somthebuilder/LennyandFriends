# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 7, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Espresso ("we," "our," or "us"). 

**Important Notice**: Espresso is an **experimental, educational, and non-commercial project** created for research and learning purposes. It is provided free of charge and is not a commercial service or product. This service processes publicly available podcast transcript data to generate AI-powered conversational experiences.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Espresso service (the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use our Service, we collect:

- **Name**: Your full name for personalization
- **Email Address**: Used for account authentication and communication
- **Password**: Securely hashed and encrypted for account security
- **Company** (Optional): Your company or organization name
- **Role** (Optional): Your professional role or title

### 2. Usage Data

When you interact with our Service, we automatically collect:

- **Queries and Conversations**: Questions you ask the AI panel and responses generated
- **User Context**: Information you provide about your interests, goals, and professional context to personalize responses
- **Authentication Data**: Session tokens and authentication status managed through Supabase Auth
- **Interaction Data**: Which guest responses you click on, panels you view, and features you use

### 3. Technical Data

We may collect:

- **Browser Information**: Browser type and version
- **Device Information**: Device type and operating system
- **IP Address**: For security and analytics purposes
- **Timestamps**: When you create your account and use features

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the Service**
   - Authenticate your account
   - Generate personalized AI responses based on your context
   - Enable group chat and split chat features with AI-generated guest responses

2. **Improve the Service**
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
   - Debug technical issues
   - Optimize AI response quality

3. **Communication**
   - Send account-related notifications
   - Respond to your inquiries
   - Notify you of important updates

4. **Security**
   - Protect against fraud and abuse
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Password Security**: Passwords are hashed and salted using bcrypt
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication managed through Supabase Auth
- **Row Level Security (RLS)**: Database access is controlled through Supabase RLS policies

### Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely in Supabase's infrastructure
- We implement access controls to limit who can access your data
- Regular backups are maintained to prevent data loss

### Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Conversation Data**: Retained to improve service quality
- **Authentication Logs**: Retained for security purposes
